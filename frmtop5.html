5. Loreland Farm resort

Welcome to Loreland Farm Resort

Address: Sitio Loreland, San Roque, Antipolo, Rizal, Philippines, 1870

Price: Starts from Php 3,317/night (Dormitory Triple Room With Breakfast - 3 Single Bed)

Loreland Farm Resort in San Roque, Antipolo City, offers a delightful retreat featuring pools, outdoor activities, sceneries, and of course, perfect hospitality! This resort is not only suitable for team bonding but also a great choice to enjoy the weekend with your kids and beloved family.

There's a playground for the younger guests, and the spa offers relaxing massages. Billiards and a sauna are also available for those looking for a way to kill time.Also, you must try savoring local and international cuisines served by the restaurants, it is a must!

Featuring outdoor pools, Loreland Farm Resort offers air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom. Located in San Roque, Antipolo City, the resort has its own restaurant.

Loreland Farm Resort is a 7-minute drive to Hinulugang Taktak, a waterfall in the province of Rizal on Luzon Island. Antipolo Church is a 15-minute drive from the resort. Each room has a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Private bathroom includes a hot and cold shower. A playground is available for younger guests at the resort, which also offers relaxing massages at its spa. Other recreation facilities include billiards and a sauna. Local and international cuisines are served at Verde Restaurant. Barbecue facilities are also available.
